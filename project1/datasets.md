
Online Retail Data (https://archive.ics.uci.edu/dataset/352/online+retail)
- How does customer purchase behavior change over time?
- How do sales/revenue volume change over time? (Seasonal patterns?)
- Can be transformed into time-series data by aggregating transactions over consistent time intervals
- Records every transaction for a UK-based online retail business, gathering specifics about each purchase

Global AI Job Market/Salary Trends Data (https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/bismasajjad/global-ai-job-market-and-salary-trends-2025/data)
- How has the market for AI changed over time in terms of job postings, pay scales, and the uptake of remote work?
- Very interesting topic, can show insights regarding salary progression
- Allows for the examination of trends in the AI job market by gathering AI job listings from all over the world along with details about pay, role features, location, and posting dates

YouTube Trending Videos Data (https://huggingface.co/datasets/jettisonthenet/timeseries_trending_youtube_videos_2019-04-15_to_2020-04-15)
- How does user engagement for trending videos change over time from initial appearance to trending list to removal?
- How long do videos typically remain on trending list?
- Records interaction data, such as views, likes, dislikes, and comments, for YouTube videos that are on the trending list over the span of a year

Microsoft Stock (https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/vijayvvenkitesh/microsoft-stock-time-series-analysis/data)
- What long term/short term trends exist trends can be observed throughout the 6 years?
- Includes daily historical data on Microsoft's stock market during a six-year period, including prices and trading volume

Olympic Track/Field Data (https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/jayrav13/olympic-track-field-results)
- What trends are apparent in Olympic track and field winning performances throughout the years?
- How have performances at women's events changed since their introduction?
- Can be aggregated to represent time-series data using year column
- Shows Olympic track and field results throughout several Olympic years, making it possible to analyze how medal distributions and event performances evolve over time
